History and Ethos of the School
In the year 1996 the school experienced a wind of change which brought new management and the Matric results for the first time in the history of the school sky-rocketed to 88%. In 1997 an apparent drop to 57% was in fact due to a subject restructuring; dropping Functional Mathematics in favor of Pure Mathematics.
The school has 1495 learners and 85 educators, it is currently regarded as a match to the requirements of a level S12 institution. The average pass rate of the school for the past five years has been 95%.
