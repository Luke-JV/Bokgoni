OUR PRINCIPAL

  • Our Principal is the passionate and approachable Mr. George Sono.
  • He has been a part of the staff of Bokgoni Technical Secondary School since 1997.
  • He assumed the position of Principal in May of 2017.

A WORD FROM THE PRINCIPAL

"...
Although I find my work at Bokgoni incredibly rewarding, one needs to recognise the challenges that approach and face them head on.

Over the past three years, we've had some challenges with the overall pass rate. When we sat down and looked at possible causes of these issues, the qualifications and skill levels of educators, the impact of an online registration process and the selection of the more advanced and complicated Mathematics and Physical Science as subjects stood out as key points.

Myself and my team of passionate educators will continue to endeavour every day to tackle these challenges and face them directly - it is what must be done to ensure the continuation of our tradition of education.
..."
