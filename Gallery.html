Our Vision
The vision of Bokgoni Technical Secondary School is to establish the culture of learning, teaching and assistance among learners, staff members (both teaching and non-teaching), parents/guardians and the community at large.
Our Mission
To develop the intellectual, physical, social and leadership abilities of the learners. To empower the learners with the knowledge, skills and attitudes needed in order to achieve on all levels of society. To fulfill out in an educational environment which is healthy, safe, caring and simulating.
